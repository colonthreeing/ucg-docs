---
title: Installing Mods
description: How YOU can install mods to your game
---

Take the `modname.zip` file of the mod you are installing and drop it into the relevant folder on your system. You may need to make the `mods` directory yourself.

Linux: `~/.local/share/Uncanny Cat Golf/mods/`

Windows: `C:\Users\(your username)\AppData\Roaming\Uncanny Cat Golf\mods\` or `%APPDATA%\Uncanny Cat Golf\mods\`

MacOS: `~/Library/Application Support/Uncanny Cat Golf/mods/`
